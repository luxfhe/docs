# 🔗 Connecting to luxfhe Helium Testnet

luxfhe Helium is the first publicly available FHE-based blockchain, and it is now live! Follow the instructions to connect to luxfhe Helium Testnet.

## Configuring MetaMask 

1. Open MetaMask in your browser and click on the Ethereum network.
2. Click **Add Network.**
3. Click **Add a network manually**.

4. Fill out the network details form. To add a custom network, fill in the following fields:
   1. **Network Name: luxfhe Helium**
   2. **New RPC URL: https://api.helium.luxfhe.zone**
   3. **Chain ID:** 8008135
   4. **Currency Symbol: tFHE**
   5. **Block Explorer URL: https://explorer.helium.luxfhe.zone**
5. Once you fill out all the details, click **Save**.
6. Now you are ready to switch to luxfhe Helium Testnet. Tokens are available from the testnet faucet. Start building!

## Faucet

To get some test tokens, use the faucet at [https://get-helium.luxfhe.zone/](https://get-helium.luxfhe.zone/).
You may receive 0.1 tokens once every five minutes. If you need more tokens, please reach out to us on Discord, or bridge some Sepolia!

## Bridge

The Helium testnet is connected to the Sepolia testnet. You can use the bridge to move tokens between the two networks.
If you require more tokens, you can use the bridge to move tokens from Sepolia to Helium.

* [https://bridge.helium.luxfhe.zone/](https://bridge.helium.luxfhe.zone/)
